About the patriots

The New England Patriots are a professional NFL team based in Foxborough, Massachusetts. They are one of the most successful franchises in the history of the NFL, having won 6 Super Bowl championships. What makes the Patriots so special is their consistency. year after year they found ways to win, whether it was a last second field goal or the greatest comeback in Super Bowl history. A lot of that success came from the legendary duo of head coach Bill Belichick and quarterback Tom Brady, who together built a dynasty that may never be matched. Even after Brady left in 2020, the Patriots remain a team with a proud tradition and a fanbase that bleeds red, white, and blue.

Super Bowl Championships

  1. Super Bowl XXXVI (2002)
    • Defeated the St. Louis Rams 20-17
    • Tom Brady named MVP
  2. Super Bowl XXXVIII (2004)
    • Defeated the Carolina Panthers 32-29
    • Tom Brady named MVP
  3. Super Bowl XXXIX (2005)
    • Defeated the Philadelphia Eagles 24-21
    • Deion Branch named MVP
  4. Super Bowl XLIX (2015)
    • Defeated the Seattle Seahawks 28-24
    • Tom Brady named MVP
  5. Super Bowl LI (2017)
    • Defeated the Atlanta Falcons 34-28 in OT
    • Tom Brady named MVP
  6. Super Bowl LIII (2019)
    • Defeated the Los Angeles Rams 13-3
    • Julian Edelman named MVP
